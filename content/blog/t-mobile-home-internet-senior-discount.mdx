## Does T-Mobile Home Internet Have a Senior Discount?

T-Mobile does not offer a specific senior citizen discount on home internet based on age.

However, there are two ways T-Mobile home internet customers can pay less:

### 1. T-Mobile Mobile + Home Internet Bundle Discount
If you **also have a T-Mobile cell phone plan**, you can add Home Internet for **$30/month** (instead of $50) — saving $20/month, or $240/year.

This is the most common discount. If you or a spouse already uses T-Mobile for your cell phone, this bundle discount applies automatically.

### 2. Lifeline Program (Income-Based Discount)
The federal **Lifeline program** provides up to $9.25/month off qualifying internet service. T-Mobile participates in Lifeline for home internet in some areas.

If you receive Medicaid, SNAP, SSI, or other qualifying government benefits, you may be able to apply the Lifeline discount to your T-Mobile Home Internet, bringing the cost down to approximately **$40.75/month**.

Check eligibility at **lifelinesupport.org**.

## How to Set Up T-Mobile Home Internet

This is one of T-Mobile Home Internet's best features for seniors — **no technician visit required**.

1. Order online at t-mobile.com or in any T-Mobile store
2. T-Mobile ships the gateway device (a small box/cylinder) to your home
3. Plug it into a power outlet
4. The gateway automatically connects to T-Mobile's network within minutes
5. Connect your devices via WiFi — the gateway broadcasts its own WiFi network

The whole process takes about 15 minutes and doesn't require any technical knowledge. If you need help, T-Mobile's customer support can walk you through it over the phone.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is there a T-Mobile senior discount for home internet?
T-Mobile does not have an age-based senior discount. However, bundling Home Internet with a T-Mobile cell phone plan reduces the price to $30/month. Lifeline program participants may receive an additional $9.25/month discount.

### What is T-Mobile's price lock guarantee?
T-Mobile guarantees that your Home Internet price will never increase as long as you keep the service. This is different from promotional rates that expire — the price lock is permanent.

### How do I cancel T-Mobile Home Internet?
Call 1-800-866-2453 or visit a T-Mobile store. There's no contract and no cancellation fee. Return the gateway device when you cancel.

### Does T-Mobile Home Internet come with a WiFi router?
Yes. The gateway device T-Mobile sends broadcasts its own WiFi. You don't need to buy a separate router.

### What happens if my T-Mobile internet is slow?
First, try placing the gateway near a window (for better signal). If speeds are consistently below expectations, call T-Mobile support at 1-800-866-2453 — they can help optimize placement and settings.
