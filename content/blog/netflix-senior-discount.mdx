Short answer: **No, Netflix does not offer a discount based on age.** There is no senior citizen discount, no AARP Netflix deal, and no 60+ pricing.

But before you close this tab — there are still ways to pay less for Netflix in 2026, including one option that costs **$0 per month**. Here's what's actually available.

## 3 Ways to Pay Less for Netflix (No Age Requirement)

### 1. Use the Standard with Ads Plan ($7.99/month)
The cheapest plan Netflix offers. You'll see short ad breaks before and during shows — similar to regular TV. For most seniors, this is barely noticeable and saves nearly $100/year compared to the Standard plan.

**What's different:**
- Short ads (about 4–5 minutes per hour)
- Can't download shows for offline viewing
- Otherwise identical content library to paid plans

For most seniors who are fine with a few commercials, this is the smartest choice.

### 2. Share With Family
Netflix allows **2 screens simultaneously** on the Standard plan, and **4 screens** on Premium. If you share a Netflix account with an adult child or sibling, you can split the cost.

Netflix has tightened rules on account sharing — members of different households must each pay, but family members in the same home can share without issue.

### 3. Check If It's Included Elsewhere

Some services bundle Netflix at no extra charge:

- **T-Mobile Magenta Max plan** includes Netflix Standard (a $15.49/month value) at no extra cost
- **Apple TV+** sometimes includes Netflix credits in promotional bundles
- **Certain Comcast/Xfinity packages** bundle Netflix
- **Verizon myPlan** allows customers to add Netflix at a discount

If you already pay for T-Mobile or Verizon, check if Netflix is included in your plan before paying separately.

## Is Netflix Worth It for Seniors?

At $7.99/month for the ad-supported plan, Netflix is reasonably priced for what you get. The library is enormous — classic films, nature documentaries, dramas, and easy-to-follow original shows.

Many seniors find particular value in:
- **Classic movies** on Netflix (search by decade)
- **Nature documentaries** (BBC Earth, David Attenborough series)
- **Easy-watching dramas** like Downton Abbey (available at times), Heartland
- **Netflix Originals** specifically made for older audiences

If you're on a tight budget, try Tubi or your library's Kanopy/Hoopla first. If you want a premium experience with a large selection, Netflix's $7.99 ad plan is the best starting point.

## How to Sign Up for Netflix

1. Go to **netflix.com**
2. Click "Get Started"
3. Choose your plan (select Standard with Ads to start cheapest)
4. Create an account and enter payment details
5. You can cancel anytime with no penalty

Netflix does not offer a free trial for new members in 2026.

## How to Cancel Netflix

1. Go to netflix.com and sign in
2. Click your profile icon (top right)
3. Click "Account"
4. Click "Cancel Membership"
5. Follow the prompts

Your service continues until the end of the current billing period after you cancel.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Netflix offer a senior discount?
No. Netflix has no discount based on age. There is no senior, 60+, or AARP Netflix discount.

### What is the cheapest Netflix plan?
Standard with Ads at $7.99/month is the cheapest Netflix plan currently available in the US.
