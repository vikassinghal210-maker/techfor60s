Xfinity (owned by Comcast) is the largest home internet provider in the US, available in 40+ states. If Xfinity serves your neighborhood, you have access to some of the fastest and most reliable broadband available — but it can also be expensive.

The good news: **Xfinity has a special low-income program called Internet Essentials** that offers discounted broadband to qualifying households. Many seniors qualify, and some pay as little as $9.95/month.

This guide covers everything seniors need to know about Xfinity internet in 2026.

## Internet Essentials: Xfinity's Discount Program for Seniors

**Internet Essentials** is Xfinity's low-cost internet program for income-qualified households. It's the most important option for seniors on a fixed income.

### What Internet Essentials Includes
- **Speed:** 100 Mbps download / 20 Mbps upload (more than enough for streaming and video calls)
- **Price:** $9.95/month — no contracts, no price increases
- **Equipment:** $0 for a basic modem/router (optional upgrade available)
- **No credit check required**
- **No data caps**

### Who Qualifies for Internet Essentials?
You qualify if any member of your household participates in one of these programs:
- ✅ Medicaid
- ✅ SNAP (food stamps / EBT)
- ✅ Supplemental Security Income (SSI)
- ✅ Federal Public Housing Assistance (Section 8 / Housing Choice Voucher)
- ✅ National School Lunch Program
- ✅ Veterans Pension or Survivors Benefit
- ✅ Other income-based federal assistance programs

> Many seniors on fixed incomes who receive SSI, SNAP, or Medicaid qualify automatically.

### How to Apply for Internet Essentials
1. Go to **xfinity.com/internet-essentials**
2. Click "Apply Now"
3. Enter your address to confirm Xfinity serves your area
4. Provide proof of eligibility (benefits card, award letter, or government document)
5. Choose your equipment option
6. Service is typically active within a few days

You can also apply by calling **1-855-846-8376** (Internet Essentials hotline).

## Is 100 Mbps Fast Enough for Seniors?

Yes, absolutely. Here's what 100 Mbps lets you do simultaneously:
- Stream Netflix, YouTube, or Prime Video in HD ✅
- Make FaceTime or Zoom video calls ✅
- Browse the web and read email ✅
- Use a smart TV, tablet, and laptop at the same time ✅

Most seniors don't need more than 25 Mbps for everyday use. 100 Mbps is very comfortable.

| | Internet Essentials | Standard Plan |

| Monthly cost | $9.95 | $30–80+ |
| Speed | 100 Mbps | 75–1,000 Mbps |
| Price changes | Fixed — no increases | Often increases after promo period |

**If you qualify for Internet Essentials, choose it.** The $9.95/month price is locked in forever (as long as you remain eligible), and 100 Mbps is genuinely fast enough for most households.

If you don't qualify and need standard service, negotiating with Xfinity is worth doing. Call and ask for current promotions — Xfinity frequently runs deals for new customers or customers threatening to cancel.

## Tips to Reduce Your Xfinity Bill

### 1. Use Your Own Modem/Router
Xfinity charges a **$15/month equipment rental fee** for their router. If you buy your own compatible modem/router (a one-time cost of $60–100), you eliminate that monthly charge permanently. Over 2 years, that saves $360.

### 2. Call to Negotiate Before Your Promo Ends
When your promotional rate is about to expire, call Xfinity and say: "My bill is going up and I'm considering switching. What deals are available?" Retention agents often have unadvertised offers.

### 3. Bundle with Xfinity Mobile
Xfinity customers can get cell phone service (Xfinity Mobile) starting at $15/month — significantly cheaper than major carriers. If you need both home internet and a phone plan, bundling can save money.

### 4. Remove Services You Don't Use
Call and audit your bill. Many customers pay for features (like cable TV packages or cloud DVR) they don't use.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does Xfinity have a senior discount?
Xfinity does not offer a discount based on age. However, the **Internet Essentials program** provides internet at $9.95/month for income-qualified households, and many seniors on fixed incomes qualify.

### How do I get cheap Xfinity internet as a senior?
Apply for Internet Essentials at xfinity.com/internet-essentials if you receive Medicaid, SNAP, SSI, or other qualifying benefits. This gives you 100 Mbps for $9.95/month permanently.

### Is Xfinity the same as Comcast?
Yes. Xfinity is the brand name for Comcast's residential internet, cable TV, and phone services. The two names refer to the same company.

### What speed do I need for Netflix and video calls?
Netflix in HD requires about 5 Mbps. A Zoom video call uses about 1.5 Mbps. Even the slowest Xfinity plan (75 Mbps) is far more than enough.

### Can I get Xfinity without a contract?
Yes. All Xfinity plans, including Internet Essentials, are available without a contract. You can cancel at any time.
